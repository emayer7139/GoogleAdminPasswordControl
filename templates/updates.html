📅 2025-04-29 Version 1.1.0

  • 📊 Paginated Logs: Audit and Login tabs now show 20 entries per page with next/prev controls for faster browsing.
  • 💾 CSV Export: One-click “Export CSV” buttons on Audit and Login logs—download filtered data instantly.
  • 👥 Admin Management: New “Manage Admins” panel—superadmins can add, remove, promote, or demote admins without leaving the tab.
  • 📈 Advanced Analytics Dashboard: Fresh Analytics tab with interactive charts of resets & logins by day, plus quick-view recent errors.
  • 💬 Motivational Quotes: Dynamic daily inspiration on the home page—powered by a local quotes CSV (no external calls).
  • 🔒 Password Policy UI: Combined Settings & Password Policy into one pane, showing current values inline; in-place editing with live placeholders.
  • 📋 Bulk-Reset Preview: CSV import now previews first 5 emails before processing to avoid mistakes.
  • 🌙 Dark/Light UI Polish: Ensured all new components honor theme—charts, tables, forms, and quote card now look seamless in both modes.
  • 🐞 Bug Fixes:
    • Fixed modal styling regression on confirmations.
    • Resolved flash-message overlap on long lists.
    • Restored persistent tab anchor on form submissions.
