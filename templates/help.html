Need Help Signing In?

If you're having trouble with Google sign-in, try these first:

  • Use your Hart County email (@hart.k12.ga.us).
  • Clear cookies and cache for https://resetapp.hart.k12.ga.us, then retry. How to clear cookies & cache
  • Ensure pop-ups are allowed—Google's consent screen may open in a new window.
  • Always click the "Sign in with Google" button—don't use browser navigation.

Still stuck?

Email us at support@hart.k12.ga.us
Or submit a ticket: support.hart.k12.ga.us
