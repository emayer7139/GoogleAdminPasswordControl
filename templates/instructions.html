How to Use ResetApp

Quick steps for finding a student and resetting passwords.

  1. Search for a student
    Type at least two characters of the student's first name, last name, or student number into the search box. As you type, a dropdown will show matching entries in the format First Last, 1000553. Click the entry you want; it will fill the field as email@hart.k12.ga.us (First Last).
  2. Confirm selection
    Make sure the name in parentheses matches the student you intend. If the name is truncated, hover over the input to scroll and view the full name.
  3. Reset the password
    Click the Reset Password button. A confirmation dialog will pop up—click OK to proceed.
  4. Copy & share
    Once generated, your new password will appear on screen for 120 seconds. Copy it and send it to the student; it will expire after two minutes.
  5. Use dark/light mode
    Open the menu via the icon, then click “Toggle Dark/Light Mode” to switch themes. Your preference is saved automatically.
